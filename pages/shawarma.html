Traditionally made with lamb, chicken, or beef, shawarma is thinly sliced off a vertical spit and served on a plate or in a sandwich. This recipe for homemade shawarma requires no rotisserie or fancy equipment, just a few fragrant spices, citric juices, and a hearty appetite!

Ingredients

  • 2 lb chicken breast

Marinade Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 3/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 garlic clove (crushed)
  • 1/2 orange (juiced)
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable or canola oil

Steps

  1. In a large resealable bag, whisk together all marinade ingredients.
  2. Cut the chicken into thin slices and add them to the marinade.
  3. Close and massage the bag, making sure the chicken is coated and the marinade.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours or up to 24 hours.
  5. Heat a cast iron pan over medium-high heat for 3-5 minutes, or until hot. No need to oil the pan, as the marinade contains enough oil.
  6. Grill the marinated chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through and nicely browned.
  7. In your wrap of choice add chicken, sauce (we recommend pomegranate molasses and garlic sauce) alongside your choice of vegetables including pickles(sweet gherkins only, no dill)
  8. Enjoy
